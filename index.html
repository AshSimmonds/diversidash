What We Do

We acknowledge that people want more nutritious foods but they do not have access to them.

We are connecting growers of diverse produce with local eateries looking to stand out in the market by embracing biodiversity in foods.

Biodiverse foods are highly nutritious, environmentally friendly and aren’t widely used.

Over the past few years, demand for diverse produce has been on an upwards trend, but there currently isn’t a formalised supply.
